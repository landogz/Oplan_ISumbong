Barangay Palangina Iba Zambales

(Vision,Mission and Goals)

Vission Statement

"Imploring the aid of the Almighty, we envision Iba as a leading municipality of Zambales in trade and industry, tourism and local governance with God-fearing, healthy and empowered citizenry who live in a safe and ecologically balanced environment, a progressive and sustainable economy under a dynamic, transparent, and responsive leadership."

Mission Statement

"To provide quality services and equal opportunities for the general welfare of Ibanians geared towards sustainable economy, people empowerment and ecologically balanced environment through efficient, honest and upright public service to the Glory of God."

Goals Statement

a To ensure adequate provision of public safety and protective services through coordination with the police administration.

b. To intensify fire protection services through the maintenance of an effective fire fighting force equipped with the necessary skills, equipment and gadgets.

Barangay Palanginan Site

Palanginan, formerly Palanguinan-Tambak, is a barangay in the municipality of Iba, in the province of Zambales. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 10,973. This represented 19.74% of the total population of Iba. Demographics Households
